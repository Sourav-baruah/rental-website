TOP FAQ's

Renting a bike should be easy like our FAQ's

* How do i rent a bike?

Renting a bike from our website is simple! You can browse through our available bikes, select the one that suits your needs, and proceed to our easy-to-use booking system. It's a step-by-step process where you choose your rental dates, provide necessary details, and confirm your booking with a secure payment method.

* What types of bike are available?

We offer a diverse range of bikes, including mountain bikes, hybrids, road bikes, and more. To ensure your preferred bike is available during your rental period, we recommend booking in advance. You can also specify the size or model you prefer, and we'll do our best to accommodate your request.

* what are rental duration and rates?

Our rental rates are competitive, with flexible options for hourly, daily, and weekly rentals. We believe in catering to your schedule, so you can rent a bike for as short as a few hours to as long as several weeks. Feel free to check our website for specific pricing details based on your preferred duration.

* what are the condition of bikes are there any measures of safety?

Your safety is our priority. Our bikes undergo regular maintenance checks to ensure they're in top condition. We provide helmets with every rental, and additional safety gear is available upon request. Rest assured, we take all necessary precautions to make your biking experience safe and enjoyable.

* Pick up and return ?

We have convenient pick-up points located in central areas for easy access. Alternatively, we also offer delivery services to specified locations for an additional fee. When returning the bike, simply bring it back to the designated drop-off point at the end of your rental period. Our team will assist you and ensure a hassle-free return process.
